GMO Monster

Students will RESEARCH an organism that is currently being modified (fruits, vegetables, or animals) by the food industry and create a colored GMO monster drawing. Afterwards, students will recreate their monster digitally AND create a soup label where their illustration will be displayed with ACCURATE labeling.
